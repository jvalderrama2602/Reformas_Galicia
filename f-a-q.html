TOP QUESTIONS

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading global energy services provider. We will strive to attract, develop and retain the best people and to keep them safe.

We will consistently seek to provide services and products that are recognised as market-leading.

Our Mission

We have to earn the trust and respect of our customers everyday in order to ensure that the customer makes the decision to choose our products and services.
There are no magic formulas. Together we will strive to create a good working environment for everyone.

Strategic Goals

Our strategy is to achieve long-term sustainable growth as an international energy services company by adding value to our customers’ operations with world- leading, highly differentiated products and services. We struggle to be true partners with our customer, and help them achieve great success.

The high quality, natural material gives an impression of appreciation, stability and warmth.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How many successful insstallations have you done?

We have completed 75-100 custom architectural woodworking installations per year for nearly 20 years. We will provide you will customer references upon request.

How accurate are your shop drawings?

All of our project managers are certified in AutoCAD and your drawings are computer generated with the highest degree of accuracy poosible.

What is your warranty?

We stand behind all of our work with a two-year warranty. Although we’ve never been called back regarding any issues after two years, depending on the cicumstance, if it was over two years we would fix the issue at no additional cost.

Do you handle after hours installations?

Our customers will confirm that we deliver their millwork projects on times, every time. If the project requires us to work after hours to meet our commitments, we will do so.

What’s the scope of your company’s experience?

We have been a top-rated woodworking company and have exclusively done high-end commercial millwork for almost 05 years.

What ensures excellence in your work?

We ensure excellence in our millwork through detailed, accurate shop drawings and our own proven procedures that have been refined through nearly 15 years in business. Everything we build is 100% assembled before leaving our shop floor.

Can you give me customer referrals?

We would be pleased to have you contact any of our clients, including Ventana, Ellis Don, Stuart Olsen, Halse-Martin, Heabler Group, PCL Ledcor, and others.

Do you build and install products to meet the current AWMAC standards?

The AWMAC standard is our minimum standard whether the job is inspected by AWMAC on not. If we failed to meet the standards for an AWMAC inspected job, we would lose our membership in the AWMAC association. We have been members in good standing since 1999.
